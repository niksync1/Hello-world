Brief History: All you need to know about cyling

Cycling has long been a reliable source of transportation. Due to advances in society, civilization , and the longer distances people commute each day, most people get to their adult life without learning how to cycle.

It is now being revived in most cites as a sport. A clean inexpensive way of exercise, with social benefits.

Selecting a bike

  • Terrain-Dependent

    Know the terain around you.
    A wide variety of bicycle types have been developed to suit different terrains. These include:

    • the mountain bike
    • the racer/roadbike
    • the hybrid versions.

    These also come with different material compositions to match the weight and the riders intent.

    Most commercial bicycles are made of:

    1. Steel
    2. Carbon
    3. Galvanized Steel
    4. Titanium

  • Riders height and size

    Very Important:Select a bike that matches your height !
